About Us

  • Who we are?

    Who we are?

    Largest Pattern Library in Australia

    At FitMyCar, we sell accessories that will Fit Your Car. With the largest pattern library of custom car floor mats, dash mats and boot liners in Australia, if you can’t find your make and model vehicle on our site then we need to chat.

  • What do we have?

    What do we have?

    100% Guaranteed Fit Products

    FitMyCar offers you a quality aftermarket alternative to the products sold through dealerships, at a fraction of the price. Whether you have a Volkswagen, Mazda, Subaru, Hyundai, Mercedes, BMW or even a Lada... FitMyCar has products to suit and we guarantee all our product will fit your car.

  • Why us?

    Why us?

    Nonslip and 100% Safe

    FitMyCar manufactures all our carpet floor mats and dash mats in Australia. We hold the largest range of custom molded dash mats and tailor made floor mats to fit your car, truck or 4WD. Car mats are all made to suit the floor of your exact vehicle, utilizing, where possible, the OEM retentions system as well as our non-slip backing, so they are... safe and a great fit.

  • Looking for more?

    Looking for more?

    Accessories? Aye!

    FitMyCar is not just for your daily driver, we also specalises in 4WDs and utes, such as Ranger, Hilux, Triton, DMax, BT50, Landcruiser, Pajero and Prado, as well as accessories for your commercial vehicle. Use the vehicle selection menu to browse the range available to fit your car.
